Chorebot | Created: April, 2022

Description:

Here is a game designed to test your knowledge. there's always a way to around doing your chores. Try NOT to find Chorebot.

  • Calculate where Chorebot might be
  • Receive feedback on wins and losses
  • Gain advantage and defeat, not defeating your chores

Purpose:

I created a game from scratch as a requirement of work from Codecademy where the goal was to use Javascript to alter the page and results based on user input and outcome.
